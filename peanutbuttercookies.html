Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • Egg - 1
  • Peanut Butter - 1 Cup
  • Sugar - 1 Cup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Spray a baking sheet with non stick spray
  3. Mix egg(s), peanut butter, and sugar in a large mixing bowl
  4. Place 1 inch balls 2 inches appart on baking sheet
  5. Bake until edges are just barely crispy, approximatly 10 mins
  6. Allow to sit for 10 minutes and then serve
